Cyber Security Fundementals

In August 2019, my team and I embarked on a project. As a member of the team, called "Team Weedle," I collaborated with my group mates Quan Sheng and Jason. Our project was divided into three sections. The first part involved an overview of what Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) is, the second part involved an explanation of the necessary programs for CSRF, and the final part was a step-by-step tutorial on executing a CSRF attack.
